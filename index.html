Welcome to the Liberation War Archive

The Liberation War of Bangladesh in 1971 was a defining moment in our history, shaping the identity and independence of our nation. This online archive is dedicated to preserving the memories, documents, and firsthand accounts of that historic struggle. Our mission is to ensure that future generations can learn from the sacrifices, resilience, and bravery that led to Bangladeshâ€™s independence.
